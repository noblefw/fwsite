Fanowrimo is a creative writing challenge designed to inspire fanfiction authors to complete a novel-length project in a month.

Registration isn't required, but signing up lets you track your word count, earn badges, and join the community.

This event focuses on fanfiction, but you're welcome to explore original stories as long as you follow your passion!
