About Us

FantasyWriters is an online community centered around the love of writing, reading, and building fantasy worlds. What started as a vibrant subreddit has grown into a thriving multi-platform network.

We engage in discussions, share writing advice, give feedback, and run regular events, including the much-loved FaNoWriMo (Fantasy Novel Writing Month), our own twist on NaNoWriMo with a fantasy flair.

Our Socials

  • Reddit: Our main hub is the r/FantasyWriters subreddit, where the community shares prompts, critiques, and worldbuilding ideas.
  • Discord: We also have a friendly and active Discord server for real-time conversations, writing sprints, and the FaNo bot.

Want to get involved or contribute to the site? Feel free to reach out to us!
