1. Introduction

We value your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected when you use this site, how it's handled, and your rights regarding that information.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: We store your chosen username and email if you create an account.
  • Writing Progress: Your novel titles, summaries, word counts, and writing sprint logs are saved to your profile.
  • Site Usage: Basic analytics (like page views) may be logged anonymously for improvement purposes.
  • Contact Forms: If you reach out via the contact form, we collect the provided name/email and message contents.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To display your writing progress, sprints, and novels on your dashboard.
  • To enable optional features like word count tracking and novel editing.
  • To respond to your contact form submissions (if applicable).
  • To monitor and improve the site’s stability and usability.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

Passwords are hashed and not stored in plain text.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

However, this site may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Fonts – to load fonts for the UI
  • Email provider – to send/receive contact form responses
  • Hosting provider – where the site and database are stored

6. Your Data Rights

  • You may request a copy of the data linked to your account.
  • You may request deletion of your account and all associated content.
  • Contact us via the section below to make such requests.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us using the contact form.
